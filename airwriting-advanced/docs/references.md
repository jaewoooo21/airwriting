# References (papers / repos / docs)

## Orientation filters
- **VQF (Vector Quaternion Filter)** — D. Laidig & T. Seel, *Information Fusion* 2023.  
  - Paper (accepted manuscript): https://arxiv.org/abs/2203.17024  
  - DOI page: https://doi.org/10.1016/j.inffus.2022.10.014  
  - Official docs: https://vqf.readthedocs.io/  
  - Official repo: https://github.com/dlaidig/vqf

- **Madgwick AHRS** — S. O. H. Madgwick, 2010/2011 report.  
  - Report PDF (x-io): https://x-io.co.uk/res/doc/madgwick_internal_report.pdf

- **Mahony nonlinear complementary filter** (classic baseline).  
  - Original approach is widely cited as “Nonlinear Complementary Filters on the Special Orthogonal Group”.

## Multi-IMU kinematic chain + joint constraints
- **Real-time inertial kinematic chain tracking with joint constraints** — Taetz, Bleser, Miezal, 2024 (open PDF).  
  https://dfki.de/fileadmin/user_upload/import/15409_Taetz_Bleser_Miezal_2024_Real-time_Inertial_Kinematic_Chain_Tracking_with_Joint_Constraints.pdf

- **Inertial and Magnetic Sensing of Human Motion** — Roetenberg, 2006 (PhD thesis PDF).  
  https://research.tue.nl/en/publications/inertial-and-magnetic-sensing-of-human-motion

- **Drift-Free Inertial Joint Angle Estimation** — CDC (open PDF).  
  https://www.cdc.gov/niosh/nioshtic-2/20055364.html

- **Joint constraints + inertial motion capture (overview-like)** — Sensors (MDPI) paper.  
  https://www.mdpi.com/1424-8220/21/21/7161
